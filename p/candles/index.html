Quality Candles from Suffolk

Sold in Three Different Scents

We currently stock Mandarin and Mimosa, Woodland Walk and Mulled Wine, each of which can be bought in either small, medium or large sizes. All of our scents have been choosen so they will freshen the smell of any room

A Stylish Design

Our candles fit seamlessly into any room. They don't burn the surface they're placed on and won't leave a trace. Simply set them burning and forget about them, as the room fills with a beautiful aroma.

Locally Sourced Ingredients

All our ingredients come from Suffolk and so support the local economy. We also use top quality ingredients, so your candles will burn for as long as possible.
